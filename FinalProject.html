This is my Final Project!

Snake

Our Final Project is the Snake Game! In this game the user must have the block (snake) go around the board and "eat" all the red circles (apples) in order to get longer. Be warned! If the user hits the sides of the board, or of the snake grows so long that the snake hits itself its GAME OVER! Make sure to note your High Score to compare to your friends. Use the Options screen to select how you want to play. Red snake? Yellow snake? Blue snake? all options are here, even being able to change the mode from slow to fast... or even having the snake go faster every food you eat!
